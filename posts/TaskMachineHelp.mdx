# 帮助文档

## 目的
1. 你是否想要做很多事情，但大部分都仅停留在计划中，一直都没有去实践。
2. 你是否因为要准备一场考试而想要制定一个计划，但永远都是计划赶不上变化而导致计划也仅仅只是停留在纸面。
3. 在准备考试期间，如何兼顾学习与生活中的琐事，如何长久地利用好每一天。
4. 已经放弃做计划，因为从来没有执行下来过。
5. 心中一直想着要学会某样东西，但一直因为种种奇怪的原因而没有去做。

这个App就是为此而造，它能让你**真正正视你心中那些看似虚无缥缈的想法，并让它们一一付诸实践**。

## 任务机器的使用场景
任务机器中的任务主要指**需要花大量时间的，需要重复的，需要花费精力的**任务。比如学习，运动。任务机器不适合其他任务：比如出门拿个快递。

## 总揽
- 任务机器App从**获取任务，到计时记录，以及打开与任务相关的App和系统功能提供了一整套完整的流程，正所谓阻力越小你就越容易付诸实践**，你仅仅需要做的就是询问任务机器App当前应该做什么？然后点击开始即可。你不需要去纠结当前应该做什么好，你也不需要在开始倒计时后，再去打开系统的勿扰模式，打开与这个任务相关的App，然后才开始。所有的事情都可以自动完成，你只需要专注任务本身。这一切都归功于iOS内建App——快捷指令。
1. 任务机器App通过**当前的时间以及你所在的地点以及相关任务的信息**，为你推荐最值的做的任务。[最合适的任务](#anchor1)
2. 你可以通过Siri或者其他便捷方式快速获取当前最值的做的任务。[位置预测](#anchor2)
3. 在开始一个任务后，你可以继续通过快捷指令App来为你完成其他的相关事情，比如打开系统功能（专注模式），打开相关App（健身App，笔记App），将数据记到到其他App（iOS内建日历，Numbers）[快捷指令](#anchor3)

## 开始使用

1. 将任务机器App比作游戏里的NPC，它的作用就是根据当前你所处的时间地点为你提供一个合适的任务，减少不必要的精神内耗，你无需自己做安排，让你更加专注于当下。不过任务机器并不能无中生有，你得先添加任务……
2. 添加任务，为每一个任务设置他们的条件（时间段和地点）以及重要程度，重复此步骤即可。
3. 告诉任务机器你在哪里，他就会给你提供一个合适的任务，调节预计时间，点击开始即可。

4. 长按"X"提前结束，或者在倒计时正常结束一分钟后，你可以轻按“已结束”旁边的按钮将结束时间延后至当前。

## 最合适的任务
1. 这是这个软件的核心，当你告诉任务机器你在哪里之后，任务机器App会通过当前时间，以及你选择的地点，筛选出**符合当前条件的**任务，然后在这些筛选出的任务中，根据每一个任务上一次做到现在的**时间跨度**及其**重要程度**，计算出一个最合适的任务。当你点击开始后，任务机器会将该任务的**时间跨度**刷新为0，随着时间的推移，时间跨度又会不断增加，以此作为下一次计算的依据。
2. 所以，任务机器App所计算出的任务一定是一个**有一段时间没有做了的，重要程度相对较高的，符合当前时间地点的任务。**
3. 不过，无论机器如何计算，当前最该做什么，只有使用者自己最清楚，因此，你可以在推荐任务视图的右上角切换到其他符合当前条件的任务。

### 更深入地讨论
- 有关最合适的任务的讨论，并非到此为止。你也许在设置中看到了一些莫名其妙的选项。这些选项用于调整任务机器App推荐任务的策略。有关内容请看[视频(中文)](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1cT411Q7UD/?share_source=copy_web&vd_source=64d14a42b8e9a57824dbf4e590cb4bb1)

#### 父任务与子任务
- 你可以在任务列表中通过**拖拽**的方式来调整任务间的父子关系。拖拽到**添加按钮**，将解除任务间父子关系。任务机器App的推荐策略如下：

|看书| 锻炼身体| 推进项目(无子任务)|
| ---- | ---- | ---- |
|三体|拉伸| x|
|冰与火之歌|跑步|x|
| 编码：隐匿在计算机软硬件背后的语言 |力量训练|x|

- 这里有两个父任务，以及他们的三个子任务，以及一个没有子任务的任务。
- 首先为了让所有的子任务能在符合其设置的条件时被App筛选出来，App就必须保证其父任务也能被筛选出来。所以父任务的条件是其所有子任务条件的并集，你不能直接修改父任务的条件，它随着子任务条件的变化而变化。
- 任务机器App首先会筛选出符合条件的所有父任务以及没有子任务的任务。假设以上三个任务都符合当前条件，那么任务机器App会根据每一个任务的重要程度以及上一次做到当前的时间长度来挑选出一个最合适的任务。
    1. 假设现在最合适的任务是看书，那么任务机器会在其符合条件的子任务中再进行一次时间长度和重要程度的计算，找到那个最合适的子任务，点击开始后。任务机器会将这个子任务以及它的父任务的时间长度都刷新为0。
    2. 假设现在最合适的任务是推进项目，那么在点击开始后，该任务的时间长度会刷新为0。
#### 有效记录：
- 任务机器App会在开始一个任务后就将时间长度刷新为0，这意味着任务机器App认为你刚刚做过该任务了，也就是说在将来的一段时间内，任务机器App并不会再推荐这件事情。不过如果你在开始一个任务后，因为某些原因而马上又通过长按“X”来提前结束了任务。这样就会导致一个不合理的情况发生。
- 因此这就是有效记录的意义，如果一个任务的实际所做时间没有超过有效记录所设定的时长，那么任务机器App将不会刷新该任务的时间长度。子任务和父任务都不会刷新。下一次推荐任务将还会是这个任务。
#### 零碎时间

|英语|
| ---- |
|学习英语单词（只需要零碎的时间）|
|学习英语语法|
|听英语听力|
|写英语作文|
|读英语文章|

- 在有效与无效之间还有一种中间状态，因为某些任务并不需要一整段的时间，只需要一些零碎的时间就可以完成，比如学习英语单词，我只需要10分钟的时间就可以了。此时在我学完10分钟英语单词后，我还想趁热打铁，再做做与英语有关的其他的任务，此时就不能让任务机器App将其父任务——英语的时间长度也刷新为0，我们只需要刷新子任务——学习英语单词的时间长度。
- 这就是零碎时间的意义，如果一个子任务实际所做的时间没有超过零碎时间所设置时长。那么任务机器App只会刷新该子任务的时间长度，而不会刷新其父任务的时间长度。

#### 搁置
- 你可以在每一个任务的详细列表中找到这个选项，默认为开启，关闭后任务机器App将不会为你推荐该任务。
- 这个机制适用于这样一个场景：马上就要期末考试了，你可能需要先将与考试无关的任务先放一放，比如锻炼身体。等考试结束后在启用它们。
- 关闭一个父任务，其所有的子任务都会被关闭。
- 开启一个子任务，其父任务会强制开启。

#### 最长期限
- 时间长度超过最长期限的任务将会无视重要程度的影响，在符合条件时优先被任务机器推荐。

# Help Documentation
## Purpose
1. Do you have a lot of things you want to do, but most of them just stay in your plans and never get implemented?
2. Do you want to make a plan to prepare for an exam but find that the plan cannot keep up with changes, so it just remains on paper?
3. How to balance study and daily life during exam preparation and make good use of every day in the long run?
4. Have you given up making plans because you have never executed them?
5. Do you always want to learn something but never do it for various strange reasons?
- This app is designed for this purpose, it can **truly face those seemingly elusive ideas in your heart and put them into practice one by one**.

## Usage Scenarios for Task Machine
- Tasks in the Task Machine mainly refer to tasks that **require a lot of time, repetition, and effort**. Such as studying and exercising. The Task Machine is not suitable for other tasks: such as going out to pick up a package.

## Overview
- The Task Machine app provides **a complete set of processes from obtaining tasks, recording timing, to opening relevant apps and system functions related to tasks.** The less resistance there is, the easier it is for you to put it into practice. All you need to do is ask the Task Machine app what to do now? and then click start. You don't need to worry about what to do now, nor do you need to open the system's Do Not Disturb mode, open the app related to this task, and then start after the countdown. Everything can be done automatically, and you just need to focus on the task itself. All of this is attributed to the built-in iOS app-Shortcuts.
1. The Task Machine app recommends the most recommended task for you based on the current time, your location, and the information related to the task. [the most recommended task](#anchor4)
2. You can quickly obtain the most recommended task through Siri or other convenient methods. [Place Prediction](#anchor5)
3. After starting a task, you can continue to use the Shortcuts app to complete other related tasks, such as opening system functions (focus mode), opening related apps (fitness app, note app), and recording data to other apps (iOS built-in calendar, Numbers). [Shortcuts](#anchor6)

## Getting Started
1. Imagine the Task Machine app as an NPC in a game. Its role is to provide you with a recommended task based on your current time and location, reduce unnecessary mental consumption, and let you focus more on the present. However, the Task Machine cannot create something out of nothing, so you need to add tasks first...
2. Add tasks and set their conditions (time period and location) and importance for each task, and repeat this step.
3. Tell the Task Machine where you are, and it will provide you with a recommended task. Adjust the expected time, and click start.
4. Long press "X" to end in advance, or after the countdown ends normally for one minute, you can press the button next to “ended” to extend the end time to the current time.

## The most recommended task
1. This is the core of the software. After you tell the task machine where you are, the Task Machine app will filter out tasks that meet the current conditions based on the current time and location you selected. Then, among these filtered tasks, based on the time length from the last time each task was done to now and their importance, the app calculates the most recommended task. When you click Start, the time length for that task will be reset to 0. As time goes on, the time length will increase, which will be used as the basis for the next calculation.
2. Therefore, the task calculated by the Task Machine app must be a task **that has not been done for a while, is relatively important, and meets the current time and location**.
3. However, no matter how the machine calculates it,** only the user knows best what they should do at the moment**. Therefore, you can switch to other tasks that meet the current conditions by clicking the button in the upper right corner of the recommended task view.
### Further discussion
* The discussion about the most recommended task does not end here. You may have seen some inexplicable options in the settings. These options are used to adjust the strategy of the Task Machine app for recommending tasks. For more information, please refer to [video(Chinese)](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1cT411Q7UD/?share_source=copy_web&vd_source=64d14a42b8e9a57824dbf4e590cb4bb1).
#### Parent and child tasks
You can adjust the parent-child relationship between tasks in the task list by **dragging and dropping**. Dragging a task to the **+ button** will remove the parent-child relationship between the tasks. The Task Machine app's recommendation strategy is as follows:

|Reading| Exercising| Project(no sub-tasks)|
| ---- | ---- | ---- |
|Three Body|Stretching| x|
|A song of Ice and Fire|Running|x|
| Code：The Hidden Language of Computer Hardware and Software |Strength training|x|

- Here, there are two parent tasks and their three sub-tasks, as well as one task without any sub-tasks.
1. First, in order to ensure that all sub-tasks can be filtered out by the app when they meet their own conditions, the app must ensure that the parent task can also be filtered out. Therefore, the condition of the parent task is the union of all its sub-task conditions, and you cannot directly modify the condition of the parent task. It changes as the sub-task conditions change.
2. The Task Machine app first filters out all parent tasks that meet the conditions and tasks without sub-tasks. Assuming that all three tasks above meet the current conditions, the app will pick the most recommended task based on the importance and time length from the last time the task was done to now.
3. Suppose the most recommended task now is reading, then the Task Machine will calculate the time length and importance of each sub-task that meets the conditions again and find the most recommended one. After clicking Start, the time length of this sub-task and its parent task will be reset to 0.
4. Suppose the most recommended task now is Project, then after clicking Start, the time length of that task will be reset to 0.

#### Valid Record:
1. The Task Machine App will refresh the time length to 0 after starting a task, which means that the Task Machine App thinks you have just completed the task, and therefore, the Task Machine App will not recommend this task again for some time in the future. However, if you immediately end the task by long-pressing "X" after starting it for some reason, an unreasonable situation will occur.
2. Therefore, this is the significance of valid records. If the actual time spent on a task does not exceed the duration set by the valid record, the Task Machine App will not refresh the time length of that task. Neither subtasks nor parent tasks will be refreshed. The next recommended task will still be this task.
#### Fragmented Time

|English|
| ---- |
|Learn English words (only need fragmented time)|
|Learn English grammar|
|English listening|
|Write English composition|
|Reading|

1. There is also an intermediate state between effective and ineffective, because some tasks do not require a whole period of time but only need some fragmented time to complete, such as learning English words. I only need 10 minutes to do it. At this time, after I have finished learning English words for 10 minutes, if I want to continue to do other tasks related to English, I cannot let the Task Machine App refresh the time length of its parent task - English to 0. I only need to refresh the time length of the subtask - learning English words.
2. This is the significance of fragmented time. If the actual time spent on a subtask does not exceed the duration set by the fragmented time, the Task Machine App will only refresh the time length of that subtask and will not refresh the time length of its parent task.
#### Suspension
1. You can find this option in the detail view of each task, which is enabled by default. If you turn it off, the Task Machine App will not recommend this task for you.
2. This mechanism is suitable for such a scenario: the final exams are coming soon, and you may need to disable tasks that are unrelated to the exams first, such as exercising. Enable them after the exam.
3. When a parent task is turned off, all its subtasks will be turned off.
4. When a subtask is turned on, its parent task will be forced to turn on.
#### Maximum Time
* Tasks that exceed the maximum time will ignore the impact of importance and be recommended by the Task Machine App first when conditions are met.
